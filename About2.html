About Us

EA Technologies is a multipurpose technology company that was created for the sole purpose of building tech oriented minds who have the vision and passion to showcase Africa as a tech innovative continent. The companies mission is to teach youths how to practically apply knowledge gained in web design, artificial intelligence and machine learning, android development such as application and game development and robotics engineering so that they can be relevant to the society. our vision is to be the best technological company with optimal productivity in the world

It was founded and created on September 2020 by Eleazer Ayuk, a young software and hardware engineer who has a couple of finished and tested projects to his name.
