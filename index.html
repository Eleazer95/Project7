Artificial Intelligence has taken over as we have it...

Artificial Intelligence has taken over as we have it. Artificial Intelligence has three major evolutionary stages which are Artificial Narrow Intelligence (ANI), Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) and Artificial Super Intelligence(ASI). The first which is ANI is said to depict machines that have an intelligence quotient lower than that of humans. We see the manifestation of ANI everyday in self driving cars, facial recognition systems, recommendation systems such as the one used by Google, Netflix and YouTube in classifying products offered and many other AI products. This type of Intelligence is termed narrow because the machine is limited to only what it has been trained to do.

A machine is said to exhibit AGI if it 's IQ is on par with that of a human.

Note: This feat has not yet been achieved. we are still in the age of ANI.

A machine is said to exhibit ASI if it 's intelligence is higher than that of a human.We have not come close to achieving this feat, we only see it in movies. However, Further research is being made by Scientists to achieve both AGI and ASI
